The Foundation used the money to renovate and furnish a large room in a church in one of the Roma villages they were working in, near Oradea. To the room were added washrooms and showers and it was used to gather the village children for activities, socializing, and Christian education. The room was dedicated in Lorraine’s memory, and that would have pleased her immensely.
