This change in thinking was formalized in January 1st of 1962 as ‘United Church Women.’ The stated purpose of this new grouping: “To unite women of the congregation for the total mission of the church and to provide a medium through which we may express our loyalty and devotion to Jesus Christ in Christian witness, study, fellowship and service.” In 1962 some unknown  hand, a person with a sense of history, began to keep a scrapbook.
