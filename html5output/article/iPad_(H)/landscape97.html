I have spoken of Jesus as the Great Exemplar but if you try to imitate Him, to follow Him, you will find that you will fail. There is only one way to succeed in that endeavour and that is to open your lives to the power of the Captain of our Salvation who has never lost a battle. How can we win? We who have been so often defeated? Only as our lives are filled with Christ, for then we shall be unconquerable. I know not where your battlefield lies nor the nature of your struggle, but I know that here is the secret of victory. Christ in everything and everywhere. Christ around us, Christ within us, Christ behind us, Christ before us. Christ in everything and everywhere. The day that we commit our lives in full surrender to Him will be the greatest day in all our earthly pilgrimage.
Bonnell, from a book published in 1936:  Is Morality Breaking Down?   Reader: Derek Morphy, introduced by William S. Stewart
