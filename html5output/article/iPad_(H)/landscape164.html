Glen was enthusiastic about the music, and his enthusiasm was contagious. He obviously enjoyed what he was doing, and the choir members enjoyed it too. He talked about “selling” the music, and even though ticket sales weren’t actually involved, he wanted us to try to show our audience, the church congregation, that we enjoyed the music and hoped that they enjoyed it too. Glen died in Winnipeg on 28th February 2017. Article by Mavis Gray November 2017 PS: Not widely known, but Glen was a talented painter.
