1989: Mozart Requiem  Mass and Haydn Te Deum  — Susan LaGrand, Mary Ann Taylor, Don Hodgson and Robert Young, (both Tenors), Fred Simpson. 1992: Our 100th anniversary — We celebrated the occasion on November 8th. As part of the celebration we commissioned a choir anthem written by Winnipeg composer Sid Rabinovitch (picture at right) —  Is This Not the Fast?   — a setting of words from the book of Isaiah. The soloists were Bruce Linney and Fred Simpson. Fred As a further part of the Anniversary we made a recording (the only full recording of the choir extant.). For years we sold it as a cassette tape, favourite anthems on one side, favourite Christmas music on the other. We now have had the whole tape digitized and it is offered here with our pleasure. The Choir of Westminster United Church, Winnipeg 1992 Director: Glen Harrison Organist: Donald Menzies Soloists: Soprano-Susan LaGrand Contralto- Mary-Ann Taylor Baritone- Fred Simpson Side 1: Sing To The Lord , Jesu Priceless Treasure , My Heart Ever Faithful , Hallelujah  (Beethoven’s setting), O Rest In The Lord , The Beatitudes , The Lord Bless You And Keep You , He Is My Joy , O God Of Bethel Side 2: O Come All Ye Faithful , Infant Holy , Go Tell It On The Mountain , Carol From An Irish Cabin , Glory To God In The Highest , Gesu Bambino , ’Twas In The Moon Of Wintertime , Carol Of The Bells , Little Jesus , Sweetly Sleep , Hark, The Herald Angels Sing
