Don’s time at Westminster Church has been defined by major enhancements regarding music making at the church. Whenever funds have been made available, he has been very involved in the project management of each acquisition: •   new upright Yamaha piano in the lecture hall •   Allen Organ in the chapel •   update of the Schulmerich Carillon Bells •   addition of new pipes on the organ in 1985 •   addition of Grand Piano in the sanctuary •   major upgrade to the organ in 2012 •   installation of the en chamade trumpets November 2015 •   In June 2017, he was part of the group of 70 individuals recognized as part     of the 70th anniversary celebrations of the Winnipeg Symphony Orchestra     for contributions to the “orchestra and our community.”
