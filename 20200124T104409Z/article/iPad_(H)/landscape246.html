The Front Steps Project (A Photo Essay) For a church building that first opened in June of 1912 we are looking pretty good. Furthermore, over those 106 years we haven't had to face many major repairs or urgent refurbishments along the way. John Russell built well! However, age brings aging and  these pictures indicate why our Property Committee, headed by Brandon Johnston, had to act, and in a big way, to bring our front steps back to mint condition. Even stone can develop cracks, and our freeze/thaw/freeze climate did its number well on our steps.
