As for me, I graduated in law and began my career as a practicing lawyer by the time Allen and Mildred took their leave of Westminster. The eight years spent in Winnipeg as minister at Westminster Church were enjoyable years from many stand-points. Allen served with distinction for about another decade as minister of Kingsway Lambton in Toronto, before his retirement. But Winnipeg was where the family matured and began to take their place as contributing citizens, and where grand-children made their appearances with frightening frequency.
