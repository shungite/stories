Many Thanks
Roy Halstead, Curator Roy joined Westminster Choir in 1951. He was 19 years old. His piano teacher told him that Westminster had a wonderful Organist and Choir Director (Herb Sadler). She was right! And he has happily sung with the choir ever since (except for one small break). He has held a number of our Board’s committee chairmanships over the years (Worship, Ministry and Personnel, Communications). He started our website, initiated our email list, and produced a monthly newsletter for a couple of years. This eBook began as Roy’s private writing project before it morphed into this 21st century internet experience. He has thoroughly enjoyed being part of that experience and associating with others who have participated in creating it. Many people! A big thank-you to the people who have contributed written material to us so far: Mavis Grey, Rita Menzies, Helen LaRue, Georgine Palmquist, Charles Huband, Mary Scott, Phyllis Reader. And the people in and outside the Church who have given us help in many ways: Rev. Bob Gordon, Rev. Don Ross, Rev. Don McIntyre, Susan and Marsden Fenwick, Nathan Poole, Curt Hull, and Derrick Morphy. A special thanks to Bill Stewart, Nostalgia Radio (93.7 CJNU) who was responsible for recording the various Ministers’ sermons, the tech work done in CJNU’s studios.
