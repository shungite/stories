This church is ALIVE! Attend as an observer at one of the 44 member Board meetings gathered around a square and where the chairmen have done lots of homework in preparing their arguments   and briefs. This is not a ‘Yes Men’ organization. Matters of Westminster are thoroughly discussed and agreed upon. I welcomed the opportunity of the Newsletter Editor to write down a few thoughts of the past years. All these years have been a family team effort. My wife and I still enjoy our membership and are happy to realize thssat our daughter, Marilyn, has taken up the reins.” R.D.
